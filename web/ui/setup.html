Gateway settings

Use either the Connector bridge on your LAN, or direct Wi-Fi blind IPs if you do not have a separate bridge.

Open the Connector app, go to About, then tap the screen five times to reveal the 16-character local API key. Keep the - characters exactly as shown.
If you have a bridge, enter its LAN IP or hostname. If you do not have a bridge, enter the direct Wi-Fi blind IPs as a comma-separated list. A 60 second poll interval is a good default.

Setup notes

              
Expected Connector workflow.

            

            

              

                

                  
1. Pair in the app

                  
Blinds must already be visible in the Connector app before Homenavi can discover them.

                

                

                  
2. Use the bridge address

                  
If you have a separate Connector bridge, enter its LAN IP or hostname. If your blinds are direct Wi-Fi models, enter their IPs as a comma-separated list instead.

                

                

                  
3. Save and verify

                  
The integration verifies the bridge connection before saving and reports how many devices were found.

                

                

                  
4. Test on dashboard

                  
After a successful sync the blinds appear as native Homenavi devices and widgets.
